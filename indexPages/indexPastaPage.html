Chicken & bacon pasta

40 min | Serves 4

Toss chicken and bacon with ribbons of tagliatelle in a deliciously creamy sauce to make this easy pasta dish. Serve with a green salad for an easy supper

00:00

Method

Heat the oil and butter in a medium saucepan over a low heat and fry the onion for 10 mins, or until softened and translucent. Add the garlic and cook for 2 mins more. Add the cream, mascarpone, parmesan and stock cube. Give it a stir and add the cooked chicken and bacon to heat through.Meanwhile, cook the pasta following pack instructions.

Reserve 100ml of the pasta water. Toss the pasta in the creamy sauce and enough of the reserved water to loosen. Season with black pepper. Top with the parsley and serve with a green salad.

Ingredients

  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 tbsp butter
  • 1 onion
  • 1 large garlic clove
  • 200ml double cream
  • 100g mascarpone
  • 75g parmesan
  • 1 chicken stock cube
  • 2 cooked chicken breasts
  • 8 rashers cooked streaky bacon
  • 300g tagliatelle
  • ¼ small bunch of parsley
  • green salad
